Biography

Meet Andrew Easton

Andrew Easton is a fun, entertaining, and highly accomplished pianist. By combining his musical artistry with his background as a prominent BBC radio DJ and presenter, he possesses a rare ability to truly own the room, engaging seamlessly with guests and compeering events to perfection.

Andrew performs classical concertos, jazz-tinted standards, and contemporary pop arrangements completely by ear. Whether providing soft background music during a candlelit dinner or heading up a lively singalong show, Andrew works closely with clients to curate a custom-tailored setlist.

Grand Piano Shell

Andrew performs using a stunning Dutchgrand portable baby grand piano shell (available in classic black), allowing any venue to display the visual prestige of a grand piano.

Broadcasting Roots

Andrew's decades of radio presentation and interviewing allow him to double as an engaging host and master of ceremonies, bringing structural polish to your celebration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you able to bring your own piano or keyboard?
Yes, absolutely. I bring my own high-quality digital stage keyboard and amplification. In addition, I perform using a stunning Dutchgrand portable baby grand piano shell (in classic black), which houses the keyboard and provides the grand aesthetic of an acoustic instrument.
How much time do you need to set up?
If your venue is providing a working, tuned acoustic piano, I only need 15 minutes to prepare. If I am setting up my digital piano, amplification, and the baby grand piano shell, I prefer to have 45 minutes to ensure everything is perfect.
Can you learn song requests before the event?
Absolutely. If it is a song I already know, I'll gladly add it. If you would like me to learn and arrange a unique song specifically for your ceremony, please let me know in advance. Depending on the complexity, a small rehearsal/arranging fee may apply.
Can you provide music before and between your live sets?
Yes, I can easily output playlist audio through my PA system before I perform and during my breaks, ensuring there are no silent gaps at your event.
What if my event timings change after booking?
I will always do my absolute best to remain flexible on the day. Since weddings and live events can run slightly behind schedule, I work closely with venue coordinators to make adjustments as smoothly as possible.
